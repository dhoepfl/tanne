Tanne supports basic keyboard navigation:

|-----------------+--------------+-----------------|
| **Key**         | **Posts**    | **Index**       |
|-----------------|--------------|-----------------|
| `j`             | Older post   | Next page       |
|-----------------|--------------|-----------------|
| `k`             | Newer post   | Previous page   |
|-----------------|--------------|-----------------|
| `0`             | Newest post  | First page      |
|-----------------|--------------|-----------------|
| `$`             | Oldest post  | Last page       |
|-----------------+--------------+-----------------+
